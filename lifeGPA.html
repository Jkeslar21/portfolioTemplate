LifeGPA

React.js | Next.js | Node.js/Express.js | JEST | Heroku | Netlify

Lambda School project allowing users to login and track a list of "habits" they select and would like to complete each day. LifeGPA will calculate a GPA for that user based on the percentage and consistency of habits completed versus opportunies to have completed those habits.

  • Served as the Back-End Developer on a team of 5 developers
  • Responsible for building out the backend database to serve data to front-end, as well as the implementation of several tests using JEST
  • Actively communicated with team remotely to produce a quality product within the allotted 4 day time-frame
